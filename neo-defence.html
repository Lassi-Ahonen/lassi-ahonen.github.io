Neo Defence

Neo Defence is a 2D tower defence game where your goal is to survive as many waves of enemies as possible.

Key features

  • Pseudorandomly generated maps with custom seeds
  • 6 Different towers with 2 unique upgrade paths
  • 13 Enemy types
  • Boss waves
  • Tile based tower placement

Development

This game was made in 5 months with a group of 3 people for a game development class.

For this project i programmed big part of the tower logic, GUI functionality and helped the other group members optimize the game. I was also in charge of making all the graphics and VFX for the game as the other two members had no prior experince with any graphics software.

Game Engine: Unity (C#)

Graphics: Adobe Photoshop
