## Project

Neo Defence is a 2D tower defence game where your goal is to survive as many waves of enemies as possible while managing flow of currency to buy and upgrade towers.

![Gameplay](/assets/img/portfolio/neo/neo_gameplay.gif)
*Gameplay.*

![Map Generation](/assets/img/portfolio/neo/neo_generation.gif)
*Pseudorandom map generation.*

### Key features

- Pseudorandomly generated maps with custom seeds
- 6 Different towers with 2 unique upgrade paths each
- 13 Enemy types with unique mechanics
- Boss waves with high risk high reward
- Grid based tower placement
