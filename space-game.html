Space Game

Space Game is a 2D physics based game where you control a spaceship trough a level with limited fuel and environmental hazards.

Gameplay

Player controls a spaceship that is affected by gravity through caves while trying to avoid environmental hazards or use them to their advantage.

The goal is to find a portal at the end of the level that will teleport you to the next level.

Player has limited amount of fuel that gets consumed when thruster is being used. More fuel can be regained by staying in fuel clouds spread through levels.

Each level has timer which counts the amount of time it took for the player to complete the level. You can compete with other players for the fastest time by sending the time to a leaderboard website that shows your and other players fastest times.

If player hits walls or other hazards too hard it will cause the ship to explode which restarts the time and level.

Development

This game was made in 3 months with a group of 6 people for a programming class.

For this project I worked on UI systems (Pausing, Scene Transitions, Main Menu, Options), VFX and spent bunch of time teaching the other group members how to use Unity since no one else had used Unity before.

Game Engine: Unity (C#)
